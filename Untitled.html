About Me

Hey there! I’m Dibyadyoti Sarkar, born and raised in the soulful city of Agartala, Tripura, nestled in the lush landscapes of India’s enchanting Northeast. It’s a place where the sun sets early, but the dreams stretch far into the night. The rhythm of this city shaped my curiosity, resilience, and an ever-burning urge to explore.

I pursued my undergraduate degree in B.Sc. Physics (Hons) from ICFAI University Tripura, where I first fell in love with the invisible threads of reality—particle physics, quantum mechanics, and high-energy physics. These domains didn’t just challenge me intellectually; they made me question the nature of everything we take for granted— mass, motion, time, and truth.

But physics is only one star in my personal universe.

I’m equally captivated by the power of ideas. I devour non-fiction books like they’re popcorn for the brain—economics, history, philosophy, science, and more. I’m a seeker of perspectives, a lover of debates, and a loyal follower of good cinema and political satire. From stand-up comedy stages to late-night YouTube rabbit holes, I find joy in wit, irony, and that thin line between laughter and truth.

My interests also stretch into the worlds of economics, machine learning, and artificial intelligence — tools and theories that are reshaping the world faster than ever. I’m deeply curious about international relations and public policy, because I believe that science, governance, and society don’t live in silos—they collide, they cooperate, and they co-create the future.

Also, give me a long highway, my bike, and a playlist, and I’ll show you what therapy on wheels feels like.

This website is my digital checkpoint. It’s where I track how far I’ve come and what cool things have happened along the way. Whether it’s a new project, an insightful book, a milestone crossed, or just a moment worth remembering—you’ll find it here.

If you’ve got a brilliant book or movie recommendation, or just something nerdy and thought-provoking to share, I’m all ears. Don’t be shy— hit me up.

Let’s learn. Let’s ride. Let’s vibe.
