About Me

Hey there! I’m someone who finds magic in both the micro and the macro — from particle physics to quantum mechanics, and the poetic chaos of high energy physics.

I completed my undergraduate studies in B.Sc. Physics Honours from ICFAI University Tripura, tucked away in the lush beauty of India’s Northeast. The spirit of Tripura — calm yet vibrant — is stitched deep into who I am.

Beyond science, I love indulging in stand-up comedy, diving deep into non-fiction books, geeking out over economics and international relations, and exploring the ethical puzzles of AI/ML.

Political satire cracks me up, movies calm me down, and bike rides fuel my need for escape and introspection.

This is just a glimpse — the full ride’s somewhere between the stars and a streetlight.
