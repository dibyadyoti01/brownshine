About Me

Hey, I’m Dibyadyoti Sarkar — born and brought up in the soulful city of Agartala, nestled in the heart of Tripura, a land rich with culture, greenery, and the quiet fire of the Northeast. I’m someone constantly chasing depth — whether it’s in a physics equation, a good book, or a long ride into nowhere.

I completed my undergraduate degree — a B.Sc. (Hons.) in Physics — from ICFAI University, Tripura. During these years, I fell deeply in love with the invisible layers of reality. My academic interests lie in Particle Physics, Quantum Mechanics, and High Energy Physics — the places where science becomes almost poetic.

I also love diving into the world of Non-fiction books, exploring Economics, International Relations, and AI/ML. My interests blur boundaries between science, systems, and society — always looking to understand how things work and why they fail.

Outside academia, I’m into clever stand-up comedy, sharp political satire, long films that stay with you, and soul-soothing bike rides. Whether it's a book, a joke, or a road — I’m always on the lookout for something real.

This blog is my corner of the internet — a collection of the things I do, feel, question, or just can't help but share. If you have a brilliant book to recommend or just something strange and beautiful to say — hit me up. I'd love to hear from you.
